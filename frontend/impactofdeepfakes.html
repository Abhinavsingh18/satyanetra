Understanding the Impact of Deepfakes

Political Manipulation

Deepfakes can easily be used to create misleading political videos, leading to the spread of false information and manipulating public opinion. Such videos can destabilize governments and cause chaos in societies.

Legal and Ethical Dilemmas

The rise of deepfakes presents serious challenges to law enforcement and legal systems. Prosecuting cases involving deepfakes is difficult due to the complexity of proving authenticity.
