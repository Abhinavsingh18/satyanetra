Recent Deepfake Cases

Financial Fraud Through Video Calls
In early 2024, a financial executive from Hong Kong fell prey to a deepfake scam in a video call. Posing as the company's CFO, scammers convinced the executive to transfer $25 million to fraudulent accounts, showcasing how convincing deepfakes can be when used maliciously in financial operations.
Source: Teneo Report, 2024
Deepfakes in Political Campaigns
Deepfake videos have infiltrated political campaigns, especially during the 2024 election cycles. Politicians have been targeted with manipulated videos aimed at misleading voters. This issue has raised concerns globally, prompting new regulations to combat deepfake misuse in political discourse.
Source: Sumsub Research, 2024
Crypto and iGaming Industry Fraud
The crypto and iGaming industries saw a dramatic surge in deepfake scams in 2024, with incidents up by over 500%. Fraudsters used realistic deepfake videos to manipulate and deceive users, leading to significant financial losses.
Source: Sumsub Research, 2024
